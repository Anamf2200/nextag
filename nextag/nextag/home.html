GUCCI

Gucci, founded in 1921 in Florence by Guccio Gucci, is a leading luxury fashion brand celebrated for its Italian craftsmanship and bold, eclectic designs. Initially known for its leather goods, particularly luggage, the brand has expanded to include high fashion, footwear, accessories, and home decor.

CHANEL

Chanel, established in 1910 by Gabrielle "Coco" Chanel in Paris, is an iconic luxury fashion house renowned for revolutionizing women's fashion. Known for its timeless designs, including the classic Chanel suit, little black dress, and quilted handbags, the brand epitomizes elegance and sophistication.
